Welcome to the **Backend Developer Roadmap**, a structured guide inspired by [roadmap.sh](https://roadmap.sh/backend) to help you navigate the vast landscape of backend development.

This roadmap outlines the key topics, tools, and practices you should learn to become a proficient backend developer in 2025. From core fundamentals like networking and databases to advanced topics like system design, containerization, and DevOps, this guide serves as a practical learning path whether you're just starting out or sharpening your skills.

## Goals
- Understand the foundational concepts behind how the internet and servers work.
- Learn one or more server-side programming languages and frameworks.
- Gain hands-on experience with databases, APIs, authentication, and deployment.
- Build scalable, secure, and maintainable backend systems.
